Welcome to Emergency Health Service Canada (EHSC)

Our Location and Contact Information

EHSC Toronto is located at 123 BigShoe Ave. Open 24/7.

Contact Canada's emergency department at 123-456-7890 for immediate attention or fill out your information in the services section.

We provide medical attention to serious medical emergencies.

Attention! If you are in need of immediate medical attention call 123-456-7890.
